### 🔬 Current Pipeline

<div align="center">
<table><tr><td>

**BHS Heat Exposure Pipeline** &nbsp;&nbsp;`🔒 Private · University of Arizona`

End-to-end pipeline computing physiological heat strain (Body Heat Storage, W) across **1,126 Arizona census tracts** — integrating satellite land surface temperature from Google Earth Engine, Cotality parcel-level HVAC data, and emergency department heat-related illness records to map thermally vulnerable communities.

**Key outputs:** BHS scores per tract · cooling-type coverage maps · HRI regression models · community risk index
